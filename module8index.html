Comparison of Hybrid Development Platforms

Introduction

Understanding the different hybrid development platforms helps in choosing the right tool for project needs.

Platform Comparison

Feature PhoneGap/Cordova Flutter React Native
Language HTML, CSS, JavaScript Dart JavaScript, JSX
Performance Moderate High High
Development Speed Fast Moderate Fast
Community Support Strong Growing Strong

Conclusion

Based on the comparison, React Native appears to be the best choice for creating a reservation-like app for both iOS and Android due to its high performance, fast development speed, and strong community support.
